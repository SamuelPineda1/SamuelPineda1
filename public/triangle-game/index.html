Marble game :D

In this marble game, your goal is to end with only one marble on the board. The board starts with 15 marbles in fixed positions. First, you remove one marble from the board. You then remove marbles by jumping one marble over an adjacent marble into an empty space, removing the jumped marble. Continue this process to reduce the number of marbles, aiming to finish with only one marble left. Good luck!

Game instructions:
1. Select a marble to eliminate
2. Select a marble to move, if the marble you clicked can be moved it would be stroked in yellow and the avaliable spots to move would be stroked in pink, select one to move the marble or the same marble selected to cancel the movement.
3. Make as much movements as possible and try to left only 1 marble in the board
